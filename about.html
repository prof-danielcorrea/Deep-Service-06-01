ABOUT US

Deep Service was established in 2021 as a result of the experiences acquired by its management after more than fifteen years of experience in the global offshore segment.

DS was born in Brazil, a challenging region of the world for operations in ultra-deep waters. However, with a Global Mindset focused on meeting the rapid and continuous growth of offshore demand, it expanded around the Globe.

We will always be ready to meet the needs of our customers 24/7, 365 days a year, in different areas of activity.



Our social measures aim to maintain our team as a pillar of support for the entire organization, taking a attentive and inclusive approach to diversity, with great respect for the human rights of all individuals.

Governance: Our board has broad transparency and compliance, through exemplary corporate conduct, allowing a broad relationship with all customers around the world.
