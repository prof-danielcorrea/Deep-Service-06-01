SEISMIC


Offshore seismic refers to a geophysical survey technique used to study the subsurface of the ocean floor in search of natural resources, mainly oil and gas.

We understand that offshore seismic is a crucial tool in the exploration of subsea hydrocarbon reservoirs, as it provides detailed data on the geological structure and characteristics of the marine subsurface.

To enable our clients to achieve excellent acquisition and interpretation of acquired data, we at DS have the best professionals for these decision-making processes.
