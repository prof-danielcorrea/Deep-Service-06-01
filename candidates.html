CANDIDATES



Some pillars define us as an organization, and we here at DS do not renounce our commitment to our Contractors. We have established some pillars in our Compliance program:

  • Communication: We at DS understand that maintaining broad and effective communication with our Contractors is crucial to building a positive and transparent relationship. We understand the interests of our candidates and anticipate this, we`re always one step ahead, so that these interests do not become a need to be met, but rather an objective achieved.

    We have all the necessary communication channels between our Onshore and Offshore personnel. This enables comprehensive communication and offers extensive support to our offshore team, ensuring that all requests are responded to as quickly as possible.

  • Feedback: We provide constant feedback to candidates, recognizing the great importance of this communication tool. The more we keep our Contractors updated on their evolution within our organization, the more satisfied and confident they will be able to work. We always seek the best negotiation for an advantageous benefits package for our Candidates.

  • Transparency: We maintain a well-designed transparency policy, continually cultivating a positive work environment and strengthening our commitments to our Contractors. We recognize the crucial role of transparency, especially here at DS. In challenging times, transparency is crucial to maintaining team trust and morale.

    We know that effective, two-way communication is critical for our Contractors. We are always available to receive feedback from our Contractors, contributing to the continuous improvements and improvements to our best practice policies, this is what moves us forward.

  • Support: We provide assistance to our team 24/7, 365 days a year, through guidance and instructions to ensure that our team members have a comfortable structural environment and full confidence in our organizational structures.

  • Safety: It is our duty to make sure that our Contractors are safe, we accompany our team door to door.

    The well-being of our Contractors is very important to us here at DS, we work hard to contribute to an increasingly productive and healthy working environment.
